CITY iLOVE

My role: UX/UI design, frontend, project manager

Company: eAD Interactive

Lead the UX/UI design and development teams and launched Baneasa Shopping City's mobile app on iOS and Android. With over 25k active users in less than 1 year after launch, the CITY iLOVE app showed results right away and won numerous prizes in the fields of digital, emerging technology and innovation in retail like "2016 ICSC Solal Marketing Awards: Silver Award for EMERGING TECHNOLOGY, Berlin, Germany".

CITY iLOVE app transforms a regular shopping mall session into a smart digital experience. From rewarding clients for their purchases with personalized benefits to promoting brands' discounts and updates, the app is a must-have for every heavy-shopper.

Highlights

  • Scan purchase receipts and barcodes to earn user points
  • Personalized benefits
  • In-Store discounts via barcodes
  • Newsfeed with latest news, promotions and events
